Frequently asked questions

What happens during a full styling?

I come to your place, we go through your wardrobe together and sort out what no longer works. Then I create a personal style guide for you and we go shopping together. By the end, you'll have at least 3 complete outfits and know exactly what to buy in the future.

What's the difference with the consultation?

The consultation is a 60-minute session – online or in person. I analyze your current style, give you specific tips, and a shopping list. Perfect if you need a starting point but aren't ready for the full transformation yet.

Do I need to prepare anything?

Nope. Just show up as you are – that's kind of the point. If you want, you can save a few photos of outfits you like beforehand. But it's not required.

Is this for men who have zero fashion sense?

Especially for them! The less you know, the more I can help. Most of my clients start from scratch – and that's what makes the transformation so impressive.

Where do the sessions take place?

Consultations also work online via video call. The full styling takes place at your home – including a shopping trip together in your city.
